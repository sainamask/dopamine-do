Drop free Lottie .json files here from https://lottiefiles.com/ to wire up
the placeholders that already exist in the app.

Expected filenames (referenced by lib/widgets/lottie_placeholder.dart):

  loop_shapes.json    - Action Chamber  (looping abstract shapes)
  bouncing_bell.json  - Takeover Nudge  (a bouncing/ringing bell)
  confetti.json       - Success sheet   (confetti / party popper)

Until you drop the files in, the app renders a brutalist fallback box in
their place so it still runs.
